Privacy Policy for SoundStamper

Last Updated: 01.25

Overview

SoundStamper is a Chrome extension that allows users to save and annotate timestamps in SoundCloud tracks. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Collection and Storage

SoundStamper collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Timestamps of saved moments in SoundCloud tracks
  • Track titles and URLs
  • User-written notes associated with timestamps
  • Pin status of saved timestamps

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and remains on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data on external servers.

Data Usage

The stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your saved timestamps
  • Enable search functionality within your saved timestamps
  • Maintain your pin preferences
  • Navigate to specific moments in SoundCloud tracks

Third-Party Access

  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

Data Control

Users can:

  • Delete individual timestamps at any time
  • Clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Access all their saved data through the extension interface

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
